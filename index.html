? How to Use the ER Equipment Calculator

⚡ Getting Started

Select your region (US 208V or ROW 230V) in the top bar — this sets all amp calculations automatically. Then choose your distro type using the tabs: 18-Way, 48-Way, or 72-Way.

🔊 Assigning Equipment

  1. Click the + button next to any output (e.g. A1, B3) on the distro sheet
  2. The Add Equipment panel on the right will show that output as the target
  3. Select your equipment from the dropdown and set the quantity
  4. Choose a Sub-slot if you need multiple items on one output (Master + up to 3 subs)
  5. Click Add Device & Enter Information
  6. Fill in Unit ID, Placement, and Address for each unit
  7. Click Update & Clear to move to the next output

📊 Grand Summary

The Grand Summary at the bottom of the distro sheet shows the total load per phase:

  • ✓ OK — Under 80% of the phase limit
  • ⚠ 84% — Between 80–99% — approaching limit
  • ⚠ OVER — At or above the phase limit

For the 48-Way, click the 63A | 125A toggle to switch between phase limits.

🖨 Printing Labels

Click Labels in the top bar to open the label print view. Labels are formatted for Avery 5163 — a standard US Letter sheet with 10 labels (2 columns × 5 rows), each label 4" × 2".

To print:

  1. Click Labels — a new tab opens with your bank labels
  2. Click Print Labels
  3. In the print dialog, set paper to Letter (8.5" × 11")
  4. Set margins to None
  5. Make sure Background graphics is enabled so colours print

Each label shows the bank name, distro name, region, and a row for every output with equipment and unit ID.

🖶 Equipment Report

Click Report in the top bar to generate a printable A4 report listing every piece of equipment across all distro sheets, including amps, unit ID, placement, and address. Print it or close the tab to return to the calculator.

💾 Saving & Loading

💾 Save — Downloads your current show as a .erdistro file. This captures all equipment assignments, unit details, region, and limit settings. You can email this file or store it for later.

📂 Load — Opens a file picker. Select a previously saved .erdistro file to restore your show exactly as it was.

Save files work across sessions and devices — anyone with the file and access to this calculator can open it.

🔍 Inventory

The left panel shows all available equipment with watts and amps per unit. Use the search box to quickly find items. Click any item to pre-select it in the equipment dropdown.

To add new equipment to the inventory, use the ⚡ ER Calculator menu in the Google Sheet (Extensions → Apps Script is not required — the menu appears automatically when you open the sheet).
